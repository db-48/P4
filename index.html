Aerial Acrobatics

Aerial Acrobatics is a type of practice or performance in which one or more artists perform aerial dance and acrobatics while hanging from a fabric.
Aerial is a broad term for all kinds of different activities. In circus, the term “Aerial” refers to apparatus that is hung from a ceiling, therefore it includes Aerial Yoga, Silks, Hammock and the Lyra.
The Aerial Arts are a really fun and unique way to express yourself.
It provides you the freedom to fly and be creative.
